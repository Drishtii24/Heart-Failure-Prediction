What is Heart Failure?

Heart failure, sometimes known as congestive heart failure, occurs when the heart muscle doesn't pump blood as well as it should. Certain conditions, such as narrowed arteries in your heart (coronary artery disease) or high blood pressure, gradually leave your heart too weak or stiff to fill and pump efficiently.

This tool uses a machine learning model to estimate your risk based on common health factors. It is **not** a substitute for professional medical advice.
